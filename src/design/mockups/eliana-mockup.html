Origin

Born from a
father's prayer

My daughter Mikaylah Aliyah Eliana entered the world after 459 contractions and an emergency C-section. She arrived with a giggle before her first cry, because a nurse refused to give up.

Her name means "God has answered." I wrote a book for her — Eliana's Journal — so that whenever she gets lonely, my words would remain with her always.

That book became the soul of this app. The theology is real. The grace is radical. The companion is built for the moments when no one else is there.

Where sin costs ten dollars, grace has already paid one million. That is the God Eliana knows.
HYPERPERISSEUŌ — ROMANS 5:20

Architecture

Built different,
by design

01
The Holy Pause
Eliana is comfortable with 3-4 seconds of silence. She doesn't rush to fill it. Silence is where people find their next true word.
02
Live Multimodal
Powered by Gemini Live API — real-time bidirectional audio with vision context. She sees, she hears, she responds without delay.
03
Scripture Grounding
Every response is explainable. Original Greek and Hebrew terms surface with phonetics, meaning, and theological context. No hallucinated verses.
04
Presence Over Product
She doesn't say "How can I help you today?" She says "I'm here." She asks before interpreting. She prays in your words, not a template.
